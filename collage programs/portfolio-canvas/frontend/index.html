About Me

Experience across Minecraft servers, Discord automation, and web apps.

Open for collaborations

I am S. Sujit Kumar, a developer who genuinely enjoys building and optimizing digital experiences. For the last eight years, I have created Minecraft SMP systems and custom scripting plugins for creators and communities, including collaborations with GamerOnTop (270K+ subscribers).

I specialize in Minecraft scripting, server automation, and Discord bot development. Over the past two years, I have built Python-powered Discord bots for moderation, staff workflows, and custom game logic. I currently support the GamerOnTop Discord server with a management bot and am building a new Discord game called RotCatcher that will launch soon.

This year I expanded into web development and delivered a billing web app with a clean UI and practical automation features. I combine a creator-first mindset with production-ready code to deliver reliable systems.
